Baltimore City Code.

§ 30-6 Award.

(a) Board to decide award. Such sum as may be provided herein or such services as may be furnished shall be such as the Board in its judgment determines to be right and proper under the circumstances.

(b) Board's decision final. And the decision of the Board in any particular case, or in any stage of any such case, shall be final and conclusive and not subject to appeal.
